sometimes we gather metric data as a series of REPORTS
    total users, projects, registrations, files...
    data categorized/summarized/aggregated over a timespan

sometimes we gather metric data as a stream of EVENTS
    page-views, clicks, requests...
    data available for REPORTS

EVENTS are recorded as they happen
                    in response to somebeing's action
                    with minimal computation
REPORTS are generated periodically
                      as needed
                      in the background



new code regions:
    mourningwail (new django app)
    mourningwail.metrics (`elasticsearch_metrics.metrics.Metric` subclasses for events and reports)



new api surface:
    /_/metrics/node_analytics/<guid>/<timespan>/ (GET everything needed for the node analytics page)
    /_/page_visit/ (POST new page-visit events)

stirrin up names...

    the basic types of datum for this metrics/analytics store are a handful of RECORD types:

        a tool may leave a RECORD of how it has been used
            => `UsageRecord`
            => "at [this] time, [i] was used in [this] way"

        a reporter (be they person or program) may publish a RECORD of what they have observed and synthesized
            => `SynthesisRecord`
            => "at [this] time, based on various observations, [i] believe [this] to be true"

        a member of a community may post a RECORD of their objection, indifference, or agreement
            => `ConsensusRecord`
            => "at [this] time, [i] respond to [this] with [objection/agreement/indifference]"
